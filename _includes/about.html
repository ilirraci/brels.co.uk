About

Our team of electricians based in Enfield, North London, carry out all types of electrical work within M25 area, from a new plug socket to a complete or partial rewiring of your property professionally and efficiently. We are able to deal with any problem which may arise and undertake electrical test inspections, diagnostics and fault finding on electrical systems.

All our work is carried out according to the British Standard BS 7671 (IEE) to meet electrical wiring regulations.
